Hanuman Chalisa

"Jai Shri Ram"

The *Hanuman Chalisa* praises Lord Hanuman’s strength, wisdom, and devotion to Lord Rama. It recounts his heroic deeds, unwavering faith, and divine powers. Chanting it is believed to bring protection, courage, and blessings. The verses inspire devotion, remove obstacles, and invoke Hanuman’s grace.
